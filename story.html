Meet Your Designer

Ilsa Khan is the founder and creative director of Ilsa Khan Studio. She leads concept development, spatial storytelling, material palettes, and the editorial tone of every project, from first sketch through final installation.

Her practice is intentionally small and highly personal. Every space receives close creative direction and a hands-on material process, so the finished work feels considered in every detail rather than assembled from a template.

Approach

Atmosphere before ornament

Ilsa approaches interiors as atmosphere before ornament. The work values proportion, texture, calm contrast, and the emotional memory a room leaves behind.

Whether designing a residence, a lounge, or an intimate hospitality setting, she aims for spaces that feel cinematic yet deeply livable, polished but never loud.

Founder Note

Ilsa Khan

Design, for me, begins with feeling. Before a single material is chosen, I want to understand how a space should hold the people who live in it, and the quiet moments it should make room for.

My hope is always the same: to create interiors that feel personal, resolved, and calm, spaces that stay beautiful long after the first impression has faded.
